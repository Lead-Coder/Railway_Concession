Welcome to Student College Railway Concession

Want to avoid standing in long queues to fill your forms. Get your railway concession done quickly and easily.

Learn More

Our website is a platform designed to streamline the process of applying for and obtaining railway travel concessions all over Mumbai.


This website would cater specifically to students, enabling them to register, upload necessary documents (like college IDs), and apply for concessional railway passes. It would automate verification with educational institutions and allow students to track the status of their applications in real-time. This digital approach aims to reduce paperwork, minimize manual errors, and make the concession process more accessible and efficient.


We cover all areas in and around Mumbai which includes the western, central and harbour lines, providing convenience to the students.

Register above to avail our services and enjoy limited disounts.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a full-time student at a recognized college or university
  • Age between 18 and 25 years
  • Valid college ID card

About Our Service

We strive to provide railway concessions for college students to make their daily commute more affordable. Our service is designed to help students save money and make their educational journey easier.

We wish to eliminate long standing queues and waiting hours for students and digitalise the entire process.
